== Description ==

[KoalaWriter](https://koala.sh/writer) is an AI writing tool that creates SEO-optimized listicles, blog posts, Amazon affiliate articles, and more. Our users love the ability to do automatic internal linking, citing sources with real-time data, AI image generation, YouTube video embedding, and more!

This is an official plugin by [Koala AI](https://koala.sh) that makes it easy to securely connect your account with your WordPress website to publish content from KoalaWriter in one click.

= Key Features of KoalaWriter =

* **Automatic SEO Optimization**: Analyzes SERPs for your target keywords and includes relevant entities and semantic keywords to help your content rank higher
* **Internal Linking Automation**: Automatically adds contextual internal links based on your site structure, improving SEO architecture and user experience
* **Real-Time SERP Analysis**: Access up-to-date search data to create content that effectively meets search intent
* **Superior to ChatGPT for SEO**: Proprietary SEO algorithm with SERP data integration produces content optimized for rankings
* **Affiliate Content Optimization**: Generate high-converting affiliate content with built-in product research
* **Bulk Content Creation**: Scale your SEO content strategy by creating multiple optimized articles simultaneously

= External Services =

This plugin connects to the Koala AI platform to enable content creation and publishing features. When you connect your WordPress site with Koala AI:

* Your site URL and a secret token are shared with Koala AI for authentication purposes
* Content created on Koala AI platform can be published directly to your WordPress site
* Images from Koala AI services can be automatically imported to your WordPress media library

For more information about how your data is handled, please review the Koala AI [Privacy Policy](https://koala.sh/pages/privacy-policy).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is KoalaWriter? =

KoalaWriter is an AI content generation platform that integrates directly with WordPress, allowing you to create and publish high-quality content with ease.

= Is this plugin free to use? =

Yes, the WordPress plugin is free. You'll need a Koala AI account to generate content.

= Can I choose who the author of the posts will be? =

Yes, you can select the WordPress author when publishing from KoalaWriter.

= What happens if I disconnect my site? =

Disconnecting your site will prevent any new content from being published from Koala AI. Existing content will remain on your site.

= How is my data shared with external services? =

This plugin shares your site URL and a unique token with Koala AI for authentication. Content created on Koala AI is transmitted to your WordPress site when you choose to publish it. No personal user data from your WordPress site is collected or transmitted to Koala AI.
